Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?

US orders typically arrive within 5-12 business days. International orders take 2-4 weeks. Every piece is made-to-order, so production takes 3-5 business days before shipping.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide. International customers may be responsible for any customs fees or duties charged by their country.

How do I track my order?

You'll receive an email with tracking information once your order ships. If you haven't received it within 7 business days, contact us and we'll help you out.

Products & Sizing

How do your sizes run?

Our apparel is true-to-size with a comfortable fit. Check our Size Guide for detailed measurements. When in doubt, size up for a more relaxed fit.

What's the quality like?

We use premium blanks and high-quality direct-to-garment printing. Our pieces are soft, durable, and designed to look great wash after wash.

How should I care for my gear?

For best results:

  • Turn inside out before washing
  • Wash cold with like colors
  • Tumble dry low or hang dry
  • Do not iron directly on the print
