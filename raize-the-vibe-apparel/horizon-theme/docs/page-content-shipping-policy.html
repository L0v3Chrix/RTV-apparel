Getting Your Gear to You

Every Raize the Vibe piece is made-to-order with care. Here's what to expect when you place an order:

United States Shipping

Production Time: 3-5 business days
Shipping Time: 2-7 business days
Total Delivery: 5-12 business days from order

We use USPS and UPS for domestic deliveries. You'll receive tracking information via email once your order ships.

International Shipping

Production Time: 3-5 business days
Shipping Time: 1-3 weeks depending on destination
Total Delivery: 2-4 weeks from order

International orders may be subject to customs fees, duties, or taxes determined by your country. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking information. If you haven't received tracking within 7 business days, reach out to us and we'll track it down for you.

Questions?

We're here to help. Contact us anytime at hello@raizethevibe.com and we'll get back to you within 24-48 hours.
